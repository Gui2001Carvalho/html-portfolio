The Best Hero Movies According to Guilherme

My top 3 hero movies of all time.

The Dark Knight

With an unforgettable performance by Heath Ledger as the Joker, "The Dark Knight" redefines what a superhero movie can be. The story is dark, intense, and full of twists, exploring the psychological depth of its characters like never before.

Avengers: Infinity War

"Infinity War" brings together nearly every hero in the Marvel Universe for an epic battle against Thanos. The film is emotional, action-packed, and ends with one of the most shocking conclusions in superhero cinema.

Iron Man

The first "Iron Man" kicked off the Marvel Cinematic Universe and introduced the world to the charm of Tony Stark. With cutting-edge tech, humor, and great action scenes, it remains a modern classic in superhero filmmaking.
